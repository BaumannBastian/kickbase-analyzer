# Compliance Notes

No legal advice. This project is designed for private, portfolio-safe usage.

## Modes

- `demo` mode: synthetic/demo data only, no external platform access.
- `private` mode: local usage with personal credentials, rate-limited and cached.

## Guardrails

- No public publication of third-party private data.
- Keep credentials in local `.env` only.
- Respect source terms and avoid aggressive scraping behavior.
